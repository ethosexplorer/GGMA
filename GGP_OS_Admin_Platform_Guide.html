GGP-OS (Global Green Hybrid Platform — Operating System) is a production-grade RegTech and Telehealth SaaS platform that serves the cannabis industry across all 50 US states + DC. It is a live, revenue-generating enterprise system currently at www.ggp-os.com.

Section 3

Admin Dashboard — Your Command Center

As an Admin, your dashboard is your primary workspace. Here's every tab and what it does:

3.1 Admin Overview

Your landing page. Shows real-time stats including:

  • Total Patients — Live count from Firebase (all registered patients)
  • Active Businesses — Commercial entities in the Turso database
  • Staff Efficiency — AI-calculated performance metric
  • System Alerts — Active compliance alerts requiring attention
  • Operational Throughput — Visual bar chart of system activity
  • AI Guardian Status — Sylara's auto-resolution rate and handover requests
3.2 Staffing Intelligence

Monitor all staff in real-time. See clock-in times, efficiency ratings, and current task assignments. You can:

  • Search staff by name
  • View efficiency percentages per employee
  • See their current task assignment
  • Audit or intercept underperforming staff
3.3 Negligence Intercept

Critical performance monitoring. This tab flags:

  • Idle tasks — Staff who haven't acted on assigned tasks beyond the threshold (default: 30 minutes)
  • Pattern divergences — Unusual throughput patterns flagged by AI
  • Direct Intercept — Override a staff member's session in real-time
  • Issue Warning — Send an official compliance warning to their account
3.4 User Directory

Complete list of all registered users (non-patient accounts). You can:

  • Filter by role: All Accounts, State Staff, Compliance Agent, Business Admin, Patient
  • Edit user profiles and role assignments
  • Suspend accounts (emergency suspension severs all API keys and portal access)
  • Create staff invitations
3.5 Patient Registry & Case Management

All registered patients with case management capabilities. Click any patient to open their full case file:

  • Patient contact info, state, role
  • Case notes and history
  • Medical card application status
  • Document vault access
  • Communication log
3.6 Commercial Nodes

Business entities in the compliance database. Shows:

  • Business name and type (dispensary, cultivator, processor, etc.)
  • Metrc/BioTrack sync status
  • License numbers
  • Audit trail access
3.7 Global CRM Pipeline

Your 24,900+ contact database. This is where you manage leads, track deals, and monitor your sales pipeline. See Section 5 for detailed CRM training.

3.8 Agency Approvals

Internal pre-screening queue. Applications flow here before being transmitted to state agencies (OMMA, MMCP, etc.). You can approve, reject, or flag for manual review.

3.9 Compliance Monitor

Real-time compliance alerts from across the platform. Severity levels: Low, Medium, High, Critical. Each alert includes the affected entity, the violation type, and recommended action.

3.10 AI Monitoring

Monitor Sylara and L.A.R.R.Y.'s performance. See how many queries they're handling, their resolution rates, and any escalations to human agents.

3.11 Regulatory Library

Searchable database of Metrc User Guide sections, state laws, SOPs, and compliance rules. Filter by category: Overview, Operations, Admin, Inventory, Compliance.

3.12 Support Hub

Live support monitoring with call center metrics: average wait time, active agents, resolution rate. View and take over active escalations.

3.13 Operations Calendar

Shared calendar integrated with Google Calendar and Calendly. View all appointments, training sessions, and compliance deadlines.

Section 6

Patient Intake & Medical Card Processing
6.1 How Patient Intake Works

The patient journey through GGP-OS follows this flow:

1Patient Registers

Patient creates an account at www.ggp-os.com. They're automatically assigned the "Patient" role. Account syncs to Firebase + Turso CRM.

2Sylara Intake

Sylara (AI assistant) greets the patient and asks: What state are you in? Based on the answer, Sylara pulls the correct program info, qualifying conditions, fees, and step-by-step instructions for that state.

3Physician Consultation

Patient books a consultation via Calendly. The physician evaluates qualifying conditions and issues a recommendation.

4Payment Processing

Patient pays via Stripe. Pricing is state-specific (see pricing table below).

5State Submission

Application is submitted to the state portal (e.g., medicalcannabis.ms.gov for Mississippi). The patient's case status is updated in the admin dashboard.

Section 9

Communication Tools
9.1 Web Dialer (Built-in Phone)

Look for the green "WEB DIALER • Ready (Click to Dial)" button in the bottom-right corner of your screen. This is a full VoIP phone system powered by Twilio.

  • Caller ID: All outbound calls show 1-888-963-4447 (your toll-free number)
  • Inbound Calls: When someone calls the toll-free number, Sylara answers with an IVR menu. The caller can reach you directly by selecting the appropriate department.
  • Call History: All calls are logged automatically in the Turso database with date, duration, direction, and notes
  • Voicemail: If you don't answer, callers can leave a voicemail. You'll be notified via the dashboard.
9.2 How to Make an Outbound Call
1 Click the green Web Dialer button (bottom-right corner)
2 Type or paste the phone number (10-digit US format)
3 Click the green call button to dial
4 The call connects through your browser — use your computer's microphone and speakers (or headset)
9.3 SMS & iMessage

The platform has SMS (via Twilio, from 888-963-4447) and iMessage capability (via SendBlue, from 645-246-8277). Messages are accessible from the Support & Comms section.
