◆ Client Discovery

Scoping Questions for New Projects

Ask these before quoting any project to scope accurately.

1

What does your business do?

Industry, company size, current revenue, and growth trajectory

2

How many user roles do you need?

Admin, customer, vendor, partner, regulator, etc.

3

Do you need payment processing?

One-time purchases, subscriptions, marketplace splits, invoicing

4

Do you need AI features?

Chatbot, document analysis, smart recommendations, auto-classification

5

Do you need phone/SMS capabilities?

Call routing, IVR, SMS notifications, two-factor verification

6

Do you need compliance features?

Audit trails, regulatory reporting, approval workflows

7

What's your timeline?

Standard timeline or rush delivery? Rush = 1.5x multiplier

8

Do you have existing branding?

Logo, brand colors, typography, style guide, design system

9

Do you need mobile support?

Responsive web (included) vs. native iOS/Android app (separate)

10

What's your budget range?

Helps scope the appropriate tier and prioritize features
