◆ Services

Hotel-Grade Inspection & Cleaning

This is the secret weapon that gets landlords to say yes. Weekly inspections and professional cleaning eliminate the risk of cannabis-related property damage.

🔍

30-Point Property Inspection

Structural, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, plus cannabis-specific checks: odor level (1-10 scale), ventilation status, smoking area condition, grow area compliance, and consumption rule adherence. Every inspection includes photo documentation uploaded to the management portal with a report delivered to the landlord within 24 hours.

🧹

Professional Cleaning Tiers

Standard Clean ($75-100 cost): Kitchen, bath, vacuum, mop, trash, surfaces. Deep Clean ($150-250 cost): Plus appliances, windows, baseboards, odor treatment. Hotel-Style Turnover ($100-175 cost): Full clean + linens + restock + odor neutralization. All billed to landlord through tier subscription at 50%+ margin.

💨

Cannabis Odor Mitigation

Specialized ozone treatment, air scrubbing, and deep fabric cleaning. Available as add-on service ($200-400 per treatment) or included in Platinum and Executive tiers. This is the #1 concern landlords have — and we solve it professionally.

🛡️

Insurance Brokering

Specialty property insurance for cannabis-friendly landlords (10-15% commission), rent guarantee insurance, property damage protection, and cannabis-friendly renters insurance for tenants. Plus our proven model: placing emergency-displaced homeowners in furnished cannabis-friendly rentals at 2-3x market rate — brokered directly with insurance companies.

◆ Legal & Compliance

How We Stay Legal

ConcernCannaCribs Strategy
Federal cannabis illegality CannaCribs facilitates property rental, not cannabis sales. We are a real estate services company, not a dispensary or cannabis business.
State-by-state regulations GGP-OS compliance engine tracks cannabis laws across all 50 states. Properties only listed in states where cannabis is legal (medical or recreational).
Landlord liability concerns Our compliance module manages cannabis-specific lease addendums, consumption waivers, property protection agreements, and ventilation requirements.
Insurance coverage gaps We broker specialty cannabis-friendly property insurance — turning landlord fear into landlord confidence. Insurance brokering is a core revenue stream.
Fair Housing for medical patients Medical cannabis cardholders may have ADA/Fair Housing protections. CannaCribs helps landlords navigate compliance correctly — reducing their legal risk.
Payment processing restrictions CannaCribs processes rent and service fees only — no cannabis product transactions. Standard payment processors (Stripe, ACH) apply.
