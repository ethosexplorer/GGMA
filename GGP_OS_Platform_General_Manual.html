Section 2

Dual-Testing Screening: SINC & IMMAD

One of the largest bottlenecks in workplace and public safety is the determination of active impairment. Standard THC tests measure metabolites that remain in the body for weeks, failing to differentiate between historical consumption and active impairment. GGP-OS integrates a patented Dual-Testing Screening Protocol to create a legally defensible recency standard:

1. Saliva Chemical Screening (SINC cassettes)

Uses oral fluid rapid-solution cassettes that read active Delta-9 THC levels. It detects presence in nanograms, proving the user has consumed cannabis within the last 1-4 hours.

2. Visual Ocular Latency (IMMAD camera integration)

Measures visual saccades, pupil response speed, and motor delay through a camera scan. If a user has chemical presence in their saliva AND exhibits measurable ocular latency delays compared to their baseline, impairment is objectively flagged.

This dual-channel approach gives commercial operators (fleet dispatchers, heavy machinery managers) and law enforcement officers an objective, court-ready screening result.

The AI Layer: Sylara & L.A.R.R.Y.

GGP-OS operates on a highly efficient **85% AI / 15% Human** operational design, minimizing overhead while maximizing processing accuracy.

🤖 Sylara AI (Intake & Support)

Sylara manages patient onboarding, guides them through complex state laws, answers pricing questions, and schedules doctor appointments. Her profile updates dynamically based on the state the patient resides in.

🔎 L.A.R.R.Y. AI (Compliance Audit)

L.A.R.R.Y. reviews business license applications, seed-to-sale inventories (METRC / BioTrack), and alerts admins of potential regulatory infractions before state audits occur.
