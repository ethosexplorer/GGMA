Section 1

What is GGP-OS & The "Front-End"?

GGP-OS (Global Green Hybrid Platform Operating System) is a cloud-based software system that manages medical recommendations, business licensing, and regulatory compliance for the cannabis industry. It serves users in all 50 states.

When we talk about the "Front-End", we mean the actual visual website and dashboard screens that users look at, click on, and type into. The front-end includes the login pages, the dashboards for patients and doctors, the buttons, the charts, and the interactive chatbot. The front-end is designed to be clean, fast, and simple to use.

Key Platform Facts to Share:
  • Credibility: Registered Federal Supplier in SAM.gov and certified as an SBA Women-Owned Small Business (WOSB).
  • Quality & Privacy: Fully HIPAA-compliant for medical record security, boasting an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau (BBB).
  • Traction: Has generated over $192,131.70 in revenue from organic user signups.

Section 2

Core User Portals & Dashboard Views

Staff and representatives should understand the different frontend dashboard views that clients experience when they log into the system:

👤 Patient Portal (Care Wallet)

Patients use this to upload their ID, complete HIPAA questionnaires, and see their recommendation status. It includes a "Driver Safety" self-check simulator where patients can verify their cognitive driving readiness privately.

🩺 Healthcare Provider Portal

Registered doctors use this to review patient intake details, log clinical evaluations, track quantitative visual reaction delays (ocular baselines), and enter medical recommendations.

🏢 Business Operator Portal

Businesses (dispensaries, growers) use this to monitor license renewal dates, track solutions strip inventory, review active staff pre-shift checks, and configure automated vehicle dispatch lockouts for impaired drivers.

📞 Representative / Staff Portal

Your workspace dashboard. It lists CRM contacts and gives you call lists and communication interfaces to talk to leads.

Section 3

Staff Front-End Communication Tools

GGP-OS has communication tools built directly into the staff and rep dashboards so you can interact with clients instantly:

1The Web Dialer (VoIP Softphone)

In the bottom-right corner of the dashboard screen is a green button labeled "WEB DIALER • Ready." Clicking this opens a phone keypad. You can call any contact in your list directly from your browser. Your outbound caller ID is always masked as the company's toll-free line: 1-888-963-4447.

2SMS & iMessage Interface

The messaging center allows you to text clients or send pre-built SMS templates (e.g. application submission notices, appointment reminders). Text messages go out from the company's active text line: 645-246-8277.

Section 4

State Simulator Interface

As a rep or staff member, you do not need to memorize the laws of all 50 states. You can use the **State Compliance Simulator** tool in your dashboard to find state details in seconds:

  • Dropdown State Lookup: Choose any state (like Mississippi, Florida, or Oklahoma) to dynamically display its regulatory agency (like MSDH/MMCP), state fee cost, local licensing caps, and active tracking systems (METRC or BioTrack).
  • Qualifying Conditions List: Instantly displays the official qualifying medical conditions for the chosen state so you can tell patients if they qualify.
  • Direct State Bypass Links: Generate custom demo links (e.g., `/demo?state=California`) to send to prospects. When they click the link, the demo automatically locks to that state's regulatory rules.

Section 5

Core Talking Points & Script Guidelines

When speaking with prospective patients, doctors, or businesses, use these standardized guidelines:

1. SINC & IMMAD Dual-Testing (The Safety Pitch)

Explain that GGHP uses a **Dual-Testing Protocol** to detect active impairment. It combines chemical fluid cassettes (measuring saliva Delta-9 THC levels) with visual ocular latency tracking (pupil reaction speed scans). This separates historic cannabis use from active impairment, protecting businesses from liability and drivers from unfair charges.

2. Pricing Guidelines

When quoting recommendation processing costs, always include estimated physician fees and state card fees, followed by the phrase **"(cost could vary)"**:

  • Mississippi: ~$150 doctor fee (cost could vary) + $55 GGP processing + $25 state fee.
  • Arkansas: ~$200 doctor fee (cost could vary) + $55 GGP processing + $50 state fee.
  • Louisiana: ~$150 doctor fee (cost could vary) + $50 GGP processing.

3. Hardship & Veteran Discounts

To ensure access, GGP offers a **hardship discount of up to $20 off** the platform processing fee. Share this with patients who are veterans, on Medicaid/Medicare, or express financial difficulty.
