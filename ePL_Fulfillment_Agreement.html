4.1 Cancellation Rights

Customers may cancel their subscription at any time through:

  1. Self-service: Account Dashboard → Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription
  2. Email: asstsupport@gmail.com (processed within 24 hours)
  3. Phone: During business hours (processed immediately)
4.2 Cancellation Flow
  1. Confirmation screen displaying remaining subscription period
  2. Option to pause subscription for up to 3 months
  3. Feedback collection — optional reason for cancellation
  4. Final confirmation with clear effective date
  5. Email receipt confirming cancellation and access end date
4.3 Post-Cancellation Access
  • Access continues until the end of the current paid billing period
