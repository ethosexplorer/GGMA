Section 1

Front-End Dashboard Navigation

The GGP-OS dashboard layout is designed to serve as a command center for regional admins. The interface is composed of three core parts:

1Top Header Bar

Contains the **Jurisdiction selector** (allowing you to change state context instantly) and your active user profile settings.

2Left Sidebar Navigation

Houses all your active workspace modules (Overview, CRM, Patients, Compliance, AI Monitor, Support, and Settings). Admins can **drag and reorder** sidebar tabs to match their specific daily workflow; selections are persisted automatically to their local session profile.

3Primary Workspace Pane

Where tabs, charts, lists, and forms load. Displays real-time counts of total patients, alerts, and system throughput indicators.

Section 3

CRM Pipeline Administration

The GGP-OS CRM holds over **24,900+ active contacts** across all US jurisdictions. As an administrator, you are responsible for maintaining data integrity and routing leads.

📞 Click-to-Dial Web Dialer

Every CRM contact card features an integrated green phone icon. Clicking this icon opens the **Web Dialer** (powered by Twilio) in the bottom-right corner. Admins dial out directly through their browser. Outbound caller ID is automatically masked as the toll-free hotline: **1-888-963-4447**.

📝 Inbound Call Routing & Logging

Inbound calls to the toll-free hotline are received by Sylara's IVR menu. When routed to an active administrator, the Web Dialer will ring. Call duration, direction (inbound/outbound), and audio recording links are automatically logged to the contact's audit card inside the Turso SQL database.

Section 4

State Compliance Simulator

To support multi-state expansion, the GGP-OS dashboard features an interactive **State Compliance Simulator** page inside the Education tab. This page eliminates the need to build separate sites for each state:

1. Dynamic Dropdown Selection: Select any U.S. state (e.g. Mississippi, Oklahoma, California) from the control panel. The simulator instantly pulls state-specific regulations from `STATE_RESOURCES`.

2. Guidelines & Limits: Displays the governing state agency (e.g. MSDH/MMCP for Mississippi), local license caps, tracking systems (BioTrack/Metrc), and direct phone contacts.

3. Official Portals & Qualifying Conditions: Renders active links to that state's licensing and patient registry pages, alongside its qualifying medical conditions list.

4. Share Link Generator (State Lock Bypass): Includes a button to copy a direct bypass demo URL (e.g., `/demo?state=Mississippi`). Sending this URL to a prospect will automatically set and lock the entire dashboard demo state to that specific jurisdiction on load.

Section 5

Patient Case & Intake Management

Admins handle the intake pipeline when patients register for a medical recommendation:

  1. Intake Review: Open the **Patient Registry** to view submitted intake questionnaires, photo IDs, and residential documentation.
  2. Telehealth Booking: Coordinate with doctors' Calendly links to schedule video evaluations.
  3. Recommendation Verification: Once the physician enters a recommendation, verify that status updates to "Recommended."
  4. State Portal Submission: Help the patient upload their doctor certification to their local state licensing agency (e.g., MSDH MMCP for Mississippi, OMMA for Oklahoma).

Section 6

Business Compliance & Dispatch Lockouts

Admins oversee commercial operators using the B2B dashboard features:

  • Seed-to-Sale Tracking: Verify that active businesses show successful sync signals with BioTrack or Metrc systems.
  • SINC Cassette Inventory: Track solutions strip inventory levels for dispensary clients.
  • Workplace Safety Logs: Review active staff pre-shift visual screening statuses.
  • Dispatcher Lockout Status: Verify that employees who fail visual/chemical screening are flagged as 'Unavailable' in real-time, locking out delivery driver dispatch.

Section 7

System Audits & Security Protocols
HIPAA Regulatory Standards:

All client documents (Recommendations, Identification files) uploaded to the platform are encrypted inside firestore storage nodes. Admins must never retrieve or copy documentation onto unsecured external storage media.

Immutable Audit Trail: Every record action (profile modifications, status updates, client calls) is permanently compiled on the blockchain-backed Turso audit logs database, tracking the administrator's ID, change timestamps, action tags, and geolocated GPS coordinates. Audit logs can be reviewed from the **Compliance Monitor** tab by executive roles.
