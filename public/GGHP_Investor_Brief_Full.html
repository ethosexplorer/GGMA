Why This Matters to Investors

A platform locked to one industry's data sources is a product. A platform that can ingest any authorized data stream and correlate it through AI is an operating system. CEYE's universal ingestion means new regulated industries onboard by adding connectors — not rebuilding the platform. That's a capital-efficient expansion model.
