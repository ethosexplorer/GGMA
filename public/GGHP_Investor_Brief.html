Three Operating Sectors

GGHP-OS is organized into three purpose-built sectors serving every stakeholder in the cannabis regulatory ecosystem.

G

Global Green Marijuana Authority

Patient and business intake. Medical card applications, physician coordination, state registry submissions, telehealth scheduling, and licensing workflows across all 50 states.

R
