Merchant Questionnaire Responses
1. Is your business Ecommerce and what are you selling?

Yes. Global Green Enterprise Inc is an e-commerce RegTech (Regulatory Technology) and telemedicine SaaS provider operating under the DBA Global Green Hybrid Platform Operating System (GGHP OS) on our production site (www.ggp-os.com). The replacement cost of this custom software platform is independently valued at $1.6M – $3.0M.

We sell the following services and software subscriptions:

  • B2C Healthcare Services: Direct-to-consumer online medical card recommendation consultations, doctor clinical evaluations, and state-licensed medical cannabis card recommendation services.
  • B2B Compliance SaaS: Subscription-based scheduling calendars, client CRM pipelines, and compliance verification dashboards sold directly to state-regulated medical cannabis dispensaries, grows, processors, doctors, and legal firms.
  • Government RegTech: High-value compliance enforcement and auditing software tiers sold to state and federal cannabis regulatory bodies.

Our operations are highly automated and run on a structure of 85% AI automation and 15% human oversight. This allows us to scale compliance verification, database syncing, and patient intake with absolute precision.

2. What types of payment solutions are you looking for?

We are seeking a robust, omni-channel merchant payment system to support our hybrid business model:

  • Direct Online Credit/Debit Processing: Integrated merchant gateway for patients checking out and booking virtual telehealth appointments directly on our web platform.
  • Mobile Processing Solutions: Terminals and card readers to accept in-person payments at outpatient clinical clinics, events, and doctor offices.
  • Automated B2B Recurring Billing (ACH / Direct Debit): Bank transfer capabilities for monthly and annual SaaS software subscriptions (ranging from $149 to $24,999/month).

5. Where is the company headquartered?

Global Green Enterprise Inc is registered and headquartered in the state of Oklahoma, United States.

6. Where are your customers located?

Our active customer base is currently concentrated in Oklahoma and ready to expand to other states nationwide. GGHP OS is built as a multi-state platform structured to expand regulatory and healthcare compliance software nationwide.

7. Who is your current payment provider and why are you looking to potentially change?

Our primary payment provider has been Stripe, but policy changes have categorized administrative and compliance support within the state-regulated cannabis sector as high risk. We are seeking to transition to a specialized enterprise partner like Nuvei that supports high-risk medical cannabis e-commerce and SaaS solutions. This will allow us to integrate physical card terminals for mobile/in-person doctor consultations, lower transaction fees, and secure higher processing limits for our expanding B2B SaaS and federal/state government contracts.

8. Is your business B2B or B2C?

We are a hybrid B2B and B2C enterprise:

  • B2C Operations (65%): Telehealth consultations, card recommendations, and wellness health sessions direct to consumers.
  • B2B Operations (35%): Software subscriptions (SaaS) and compliance widgets sold to medical cannabis dispensaries, grows, processors, doctors, law firms, and state regulatory offices.
9. How many employees do you have in the US/Canada?

Because our platform operations are built to run 85% on AI automation and 15% on human oversight, we operate with an extremely lean core corporate and executive team in the United States. Our proprietary AI agents (Sylara and L.A.R.R.Y.) handle the vast majority of customer intake, compliance tracking, and administrative tasks, while a small network of contracted clinical professionals, developers, and support staff provide the human validation (15%) required for clinical compliance and oversight.
