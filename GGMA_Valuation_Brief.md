## The Problem Operators Face Today

Operating a licensed cannabis business means navigating a minefield of compliance, tax, and banking barriers:

1. **Catastrophic Metrc/BioTrack Risk**: A single clerk error in inventory entry can trigger license suspension or daily state fines, halting operations instantly.
2. **280E and Cash Penalties**: Massive cash-handling overhead, high vaulting costs, and security risks are compounded by the lack of traditional banking access and credit card processing.
3. **Fragmented Telehealth & Intake**: Patient registration, doctor certifications, and state registry submissions are manual, slow, and disconnected from retail inventory systems.
4. **Logistical Exposure**: Secure transport vehicles and delivery drivers face extreme risk from local law enforcement due to a lack of real-time, cross-jurisdictional verification tools.
