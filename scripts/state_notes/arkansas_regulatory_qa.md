## Program Overview
- **Legal Basis:** Arkansas Medical Marijuana Amendment of 2016 (Issue 6, Amendment 98)
- **Program Type:** MEDICAL ONLY — No recreational/adult-use program
- **Regulatory Bodies:**
  - **Arkansas Medical Marijuana Commission (AMMC)** — licensing, oversight (under DFA)
  - **Arkansas Alcoholic Beverage Control (ABC)** — enforcement
  - **Arkansas Department of Health (ADH)** — patient registry
- **Patient Registry Contact:** 501-682-4982
- **Patient Portal:** https://mmj.adh.arkansas.gov/
- **DFA/AMMC:** https://www.dfa.arkansas.gov/office/medical-marijuana-commission/
- **Processing Time:** Allow up to 14 days from receipt of application + payment
- **System Maintenance:** Payment processing deactivated Sundays 6pm-12am

### License Caps — Highly Restrictive Market
- **Cultivators: CAPPED at 8 licenses** (no new applications accepted)
- **Dispensaries: CAPPED at ~40 licenses** (no new applications accepted)
- **Processors: NO CAP** — rolling applications accepted
- **Transporters: Rolling applications** accepted
- This is one of the most restrictive state markets in the US

### Purchase Tracking
- Allotment purchases now tracked to fourteen days **to the minute** of purchase
- 2.5 oz per 14-day rolling period

- Cancer
- Glaucoma
- Positive status for HIV/AIDS
- Hepatitis C
- Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS)
- Tourette's Syndrome
- Crohn's Disease
- Ulcerative Colitis
- Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
- Severe Arthritis
- Fibromyalgia
- Alzheimer's Disease
- Cachexia or Wasting Syndrome
- Peripheral Neuropathy
- Intractable Pain (unrelieved by standard treatment)
- Severe Nausea
- Seizures (including Epilepsy)
- Severe/Persistent Muscle Spasms (including Multiple Sclerosis)
- Any condition approved by the ADH

## Intake Application Setup

### 🏢 BUSINESS Application Checklist
#### Processor (Open — Rolling Applications)
1. Download Processor Application from DFA: https://www.dfa.arkansas.gov/wp-content/uploads/Processor_ApplicationandInstructions.pdf
2. Complete all sections of the application
3. Submit Arkansas State Police background check for ALL principals/owners
   - Instructions: https://www.dfa.arkansas.gov/wp-content/uploads/MMC_ASP_InstructionSheet.docx
   - Background Application: https://www.dfa.arkansas.gov/wp-content/uploads/MMC_ASP_BackgroundApplication.pdf
4. Submit fingerprints for all key personnel
5. Provide proof of Arkansas business registration
6. Submit facility floor plans and security plans
7. Pay application fee (check current DFA fee schedule)
8. AMMC review and approval process
9. Facility inspection prior to operational approval

#### Transporter (Open — Rolling Applications)
1. Download Transporter Application: https://www.dfa.arkansas.gov/wp-content/uploads/Transporter_ApplicationandInstructions.pdf
2. Complete application + background checks for all personnel
3. Submit vehicle information and transport security plan
4. AMMC review and approval

   - Transfer of Location: https://www.dfa.arkansas.gov/wp-content/uploads/Transfer_of_Location_Application_Cultivation.pdf

### 🩺 PATIENT / CAREGIVER Application Checklist
1. Must be Arkansas resident with valid state ID
2. Must have qualifying medical condition (see list above)
3. Obtain physician certification of qualifying condition
4. **NEW Patients:** Go to https://mmj.adh.arkansas.gov/ → click "Patient Registration"
5. **Existing Patients:** Go to portal → click "Login"
6. If renewing with a NEW email address, call 501-682-4982 FIRST
7. If you forgot your password, use "Reset Password" on login page
8. Complete online application with:
   - Personal identification information
   - Physician certification details
   - Photo for ID card
9. Submit payment online (payment deactivated Sundays 6pm-midnight)
10. Allow up to 14 days for processing (beginning day after receipt)
11. **Patient Registry Card** issued upon approval
12. **Possession Limit:** 2.5 ounces per 14-day rolling period (tracked to the minute)
13. **Caregiver:**
    - Must be 21+ years old
    - Apply through ADH patient registry portal
    - Background check required
    - Designated by patient
    - Can assist only their designated patient(s)
14. **Renewal:** Must renew before expiration through online portal
15. **No Home Cultivation** — patients must purchase from licensed dispensaries only
16. Forms available at: https://mmj.adh.arkansas.gov/?action=index&subaction=forms

### 👨‍⚕️ PROVIDER (Physician) Registration Checklist
1. Must hold active Arkansas medical license (MD or DO)
2. Must be in good standing with Arkansas State Medical Board
3. Review Amendment 98 certification requirements
4. Physician evaluates patient for qualifying medical condition
5. Issues written certification if condition is met
6. Certification requirements:
   - Establish bona fide physician-patient relationship
   - Review patient's complete medical history
   - Conduct physical examination
   - Document qualifying condition in medical record
   - Certify that benefits of medical marijuana outweigh risks
7. No separate state cannabis-specific physician registration required
8. Physician can certify any number of patients
9. Cannot have financial interest in dispensary or cultivation facility
10. Must maintain records per Arkansas State Medical Board standards
11. Certification submitted by patient to ADH via online registry

## Frequently Asked Questions (Q&A)

### General
- **Q: Is recreational marijuana legal in Arkansas?**
  A: NO. Arkansas is MEDICAL ONLY. Issue 4 (2022 recreational ballot measure) failed with 44% yes.

- **Q: Can I grow marijuana at home?**
  A: NO. Home cultivation is NOT permitted under Amendment 98. All marijuana must be purchased from licensed dispensaries.

- **Q: How much can I purchase?**
  A: 2.5 ounces per 14-day rolling period, tracked to the minute of each purchase.

- **Q: Can I get marijuana delivered?**
  A: Licensed transporters exist, but direct-to-patient delivery depends on dispensary services. Check with your dispensary.

### Business
- **Q: Can I open a new dispensary?**
  A: No — dispensary licenses are capped at ~40 and are not accepting new applications. You can pursue a Transfer of Ownership of an existing license.

- **Q: Can I become a processor?**
  A: YES — processor licenses have no cap. Download the application from the DFA website.

- **Q: What background checks are required?**
  A: Arkansas State Police background checks required for all principals and key personnel.

- **Q: Is a performance bond required?**
  A: Yes — both cultivation facilities and dispensaries require performance bonds.

### Patients
- **Q: How do I apply for a medical marijuana card?**
  A: Online at https://mmj.adh.arkansas.gov/ — click Patient Registration, complete the form, submit physician certification and payment.

- **Q: How long does processing take?**
  A: Up to 14 days from the day after application and payment are received.

- **Q: What if I forget my password?**
  A: Use the "Reset Password" button on the login page. If renewing with a new email, call 501-682-4982 first.

## Key Compliance Notes
- **Medical Only** — no recreational program exists
- **No Home Cultivation** — all product must come from licensed dispensaries
- **License Caps** — extremely limited market entry for dispensaries and cultivators
- **8-Zone System** — dispensaries allocated across geographic zones for statewide access
- **Performance Bonds** required for cultivation and dispensary licensees
- **ABC Enforcement** — Alcoholic Beverage Control handles compliance enforcement
- **14-Day Allotment** tracked to the minute — exceeding limits is a violation
