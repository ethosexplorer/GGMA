# Alaska AMCO — Regulatory Q&A Reference
Source: https://www.commerce.alaska.gov/web/amco/MarijuanaFAQs
Medical: https://health.alaska.gov/media/ocjibnfh/medicalmarijuana.pdf

## Program Overview
- **Legal Basis:** AS 17.38, passed by citizen initiative Nov 4, 2014
- **Regulatory Body:** Marijuana Control Board (MCB), under AMCO
- **Tracking System:** METRC (Franwell) — real-time RFID/barcode seed-to-sale
- **Contact:** marijuana.licensing@alaska.gov | (907) 269-0350
- **Office:** 550 W 7th Ave, Suite 1600, Anchorage, AK 99501
- **Hours:** Mon-Fri 9am-4pm (closed Wed for in-person, closed noon-1pm)

## License Types (6 total)
1. **Retail Marijuana Store** — consumer sales
2. **Standard Marijuana Cultivation Facility** — full-scale grow
3. **Limited Marijuana Cultivation Facility** — small-scale grow
4. **Marijuana Product Manufacturing Facility** — edibles/products
5. **Marijuana Concentrate Manufacturing Facility** — extracts
6. **Marijuana Testing Facility** — independent, cannot hold other license types

## Key Regulations
- **Residency Required:** All persons with financial interest must be AK residents (PFD eligible)
- **No License Cap:** State has no limit; local govts can restrict time/place/manner/number
- **Premises-Based:** Must secure location BEFORE applying; need deed or lease
- **Buffer Zone:** 500 ft from schools, youth/recreation centers, religious buildings, correctional facilities
- **No Delivery:** Not permitted under AS 17.38 or 3 AAC 306
- **No Dispensary Term:** Alaska uses "Retail Marijuana Store" — not "dispensary"
- **Multiple Licenses OK:** Except testing facilities (must be independent)
- **Application Timeline:** Plan for 4-6 month process
- **Local Opt-Out:** Communities can opt out by ordinance or petition election (3 AAC 306.200)
- **METRC Required:** All licensees must use METRC; internet access mandatory

## Personal Use Rules
- **Age:** 21+ only
- **Possession:** Up to 1 oz for personal use
- **Home Grow:** 6 plants max (3 mature/flowering) per person
- **Per Dwelling:** 12 plants max (6 mature) regardless of residents
- **No Cooperative Grows:** Cannot combine limits or proxy for others
- **Transport:** 1 oz or less; can give 1 oz to another adult

## Commercial Transport
- **No Weight Limit** for licensed transfers between businesses
- **Must Be Accompanied** by person with valid Marijuana Handler Permit
- **Sealed/Locked:** Must be in sealed package, locked storage compartment
- **Cannot Ship Unaccompanied** (no goldstreak/mail)

## Handler Permits
- Take approved course → bring certificate + photo ID to AMCO → $50 fee
- Required for all marijuana establishment employees

## Application Process
1. Secure premises location (deed/lease)
2. Initiate application online (myAlaska account + AK business license required)
3. Post Public Notice at premises + nearby location for 10 days
4. Notify local government + community council
5. Run newspaper ad 3 consecutive weeks
6. Submit supplemental forms + operating plan
7. Submit fingerprint cards (taken within last 12 months)
8. Submit payment (check/money order only — no credit cards)
9. AMCO review → deemed complete → 60-day local govt protest period
10. 30-day public objection period
11. Board consideration within 90 days of completion

## DUI / Impairment
- Marijuana remains controlled substance under Title 11
- AS 28.35.030: Illegal to drive under influence of any controlled substance
- No change from legalization initiative

## Pesticides
- Regulated by Dept of Environmental Conservation, Division of Environmental Health
- See: https://dec.alaska.gov/eh/pest/
