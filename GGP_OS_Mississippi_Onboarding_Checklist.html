☐ Patient Intake Readiness Mississippi medical card flow
  • Know the pricing: Doctor ~$150 + GGP $55 + State $25 = ~$230 (cost could vary)
  • Know the process: Physician → MMCP Portal → $25 fee → 15-30 days → Digital card
  • Know qualifying conditions: 22 conditions (cancer, epilepsy, PTSD, chronic pain, MS, etc.)
  • Know the state portal: medicalcannabis.ms.gov (patient registration)
  • Hardship discount: Up to $20 off GGP fee for Medicaid/Medicare/VA/financial hardship
